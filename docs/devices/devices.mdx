## Helium Devices
### Find ready to use devices or learn how to develop your own. 

The Helium network supports any LoRaWAN capable device meeting the [v1.0.2 specification](https://lora-alliance.org/sites/default/files/2018-05/lorawan1_0_2-20161012_1398_1.pdf).
